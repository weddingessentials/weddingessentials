The woman behind Wedding Essentials

I started this because I kept watching brides disappear inside their own weddings.

Work with me

Every detail was perfect. The room, the flowers, the dress. And somehow — in the middle of all of it — the bride was managing rather than experiencing. Present in body, but slightly outside of the moment.

I built Wedding Essentials to close that gap. Between what a wedding looks like and what it feels like. Between the bride who hopes it goes well and the one who knows it will. Between the day that passes in a blur and the one that is lived — completely, consciously, in the body — from the first moment to the last.

My name is Mira. I am a wedding expert, a bridal confidence coach, and the founder of Wedding Essentials. I work with brides across the Gulf and beyond — in grand ballrooms and intimate gardens, in full zaffa productions and quiet ceremonies.

What I know after years of this work is simple: the most important person at a wedding is the bride. And the most important preparation she can do has nothing to do with the décor.

It has everything to do with her.
